---
title: Deleting a Firmware Bundle version
---

![](https://usercontent.clueso.io/1f4455e0-789e-4beb-8cd9-4dac96bc47e4/a899a100-180f-45fd-8777-8f33d2b32b2c/2239feb2-dc6e-480b-ad2e-6307786d5f37/images/b9f1965c-5f0f-4994-927a-7c2337603ffa.gif)

To delete a Firmware Bundle version in Bytebeam console,

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Actions Tab">
    Click on the **Actions** tab
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Inventory Tab and Access Firmware Files Tab">
    Navigate to the **Firmware Files** tab by clicking on Inventory section
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Firmware Bundles Tab and Initiate Firmware Bundle Deletion">
    Click on **Firmware Files** tab, click on the **Firmware Bundles** tab and locate the **Firmware Bundle** to delete
</Step>
<Step title="Click on Delete icon and Confirm Deletion">
    Click on **Delete** icon against the located bundle version and, enter the bundle version and click on **Confirm** button.
</Step>
</Steps>
