title: Viewing a Geofence Configuration version
---

To view a Geofence Configuration version in Bytebeam console,

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Actions Tab">
    Click on the **Actions** tab
  </Step>
 <Step title="Go to Inventory Tab and Access Geofence Configurations Tab">
    Navigate to the **Geofence Configurations** tab by clicking on Inventory section
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate the desired Geofence Configuration version ">
    Click on **Geofence Configurations** tab and locate the desired **Geofence Configuration version**
</Step>
<Step title="Click on Eye icon">
    Click on **Eye** icon against the located version to view it.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>You can edit the Geofence Configuration version by clicking on Edit button and [follow the instructions](/platform-guide/actions/inventory-management/geofence-configurations/editing-a-geofence-configuration)</Note>

You can view even the deactivated Geofence Configuration versions.

To view a deactivated Geofence Configuration version,

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Actions Tab">
    Click on the **Actions** tab
  </Step>
 <Step title="Go to Inventory Tab and Access Geofence Configurations Tab">
    Navigate to the **Geofence Configurations** tab by clicking on Inventory section
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable 'Show Deactivated' toggle">
    Click on **Geofence Configurations** tab and click on **Show Deactivated** toggle.
</Step>
  <Step title="Locate the desired Geofence Configuration version ">
    Scroll to the desired **Geofence Configuration version**
</Step>
<Step title="Click on Eye icon">
    Click on **Eye** icon against the located deactivated version to view it.
</Step>
</Steps>
