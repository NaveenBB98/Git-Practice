To delete a dashboard in the Bytebeam console, follow these steps carefully:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboards">
    Go to the **Dashboards** tab in the Bytebeam console to view the list of available dashboards.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on the Delete Icon">
    Locate the dashboard you wish to delete and click on the **Trash ðŸ—‘ icon** next to it.  
    ![](/assets/1a1o9SOOKUHytB8EUm5x__screenshot-29.jpeg)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter the Dashboard Name">
    - A confirmation modal will appear.  
    - Enter the **exact name** of the dashboard you wish to delete in the input field to confirm.  
    ![](/assets/cMleqz8fYFkTE21aGfmyr_screenshot-32.jpeg)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Confirm">
    Click on the **Confirm** button to delete the dashboard. This action cannot be undone.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Deleting a dashboard is an **irreversible action**. Ensure that you have selected the correct dashboard before proceeding.
</Warning>
