title: Deleting a DBC Parser
---

![](https://usercontent.clueso.io/1f4455e0-789e-4beb-8cd9-4dac96bc47e4/246efdc5-ecb7-4ee7-9b4b-c1d0da52923f/00129675-d005-494d-8dc9-71cdbebc0632/images/54358279-fb41-47d7-813d-fe1c5a19f6f1.gif)

To delete the existing DBC parser in Bytebeam console,

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Settings Tab">
    Navigate to the **Settings Tab** by clicking on your username at the top right
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to DBC Parsers Tab and Initiate DBC Parser Deletion">
    Navigate to the **DBC Parsers** tab and click on the **trash**(ðŸ—‘) icon against the target DBC parser
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Details">
    Enter the DBC parser name and click on **Submit** button to delete it.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>When a DBC Parser is running, it cannot be deleted until it is stopped.</Warning>
