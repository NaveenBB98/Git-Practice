title: Deleting a Device Component
---

![](https://usercontent.clueso.io/1f4455e0-789e-4beb-8cd9-4dac96bc47e4/bba939b0-1a8d-42f2-8d80-b469918eb165/c23c2f44-7062-4517-9c0a-cef1c1d66c74/images/19a04af0-f755-491d-b81c-3ab9862587f6.gif)

To delete the existing device component in Bytebeam console,

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Settings Tab">
    Navigate to the **Settings Tab** by clicking on your username at the top right
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Device Components Tab and Initiate Device Component Deletion">
    Navigate to the **Device Components** tab and click on the **trash**(ðŸ—‘) icon against the target device component
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Details">
    Enter the device component name and click on **Submit** button to delete the device component.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>When a device component has firmware files uploaded, it cannot be deleted until all the firmware files are deactivated for that component.</Note>
