To create a new Metadata column,

**Step 1: **Navigate to the ***Settings Tab*** by clicking on the user name at the top right

![](/assets/5OY5oDz-qJzz4AFQfyQZ4_2023-10-2501-13.png)

**Step 2:** Navigate to the ***Metadata Tab***

![](/assets/lT62bRMBAxpv1vdqL0DQt_screenshot-15.jpg)

**Step 3: **Click on the *Create Metadata*** **button

![](/assets/NOIB2O3gxC-Zaf5JYUChp_screenshot-16.jpg)

**Step 4:** Enter the desired Metadata Key, and click on the *Submit *button

![](/assets/sCyJCTqPwO1C0zuDlkp23_screenshot-21.jpg)



You will find the new *Metadata key* in the table on the*** Metadata*** tab.

![](/assets/o35qY9cUKN0caJf595o-U_screenshot-20.jpg)
