To clone an existing role in Bytebeam console,

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Settings Tab">
    Navigate to the **Settings Tab** by clicking on your username at the top right
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Roles Tab and Initiate Role Edit">
    Navigate to the **Roles** tab, locate the role and click on the **clone** icon against it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Details">
    Change the details from the [Tabs](/platform-guide/roles/roles-sections) and click on **Submit** button to clone the role.
</Step>
</Steps>
