To create a new User,

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Settings Tab">
    Navigate to the **Settings Tab** by clicking on your username at the top right
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Users Tab and Initiate User Creation">
    Navigate to the **Users** tab and click on the **Create User** button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Details">
    Enter the username, email, select a role from dropdown and click on **Submit** button to create the role.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Important Notes

<Note> Creating a new User will trigger a welcome e-mail to the given e-mail address.</Note>

<Note> Only admin users have the ability to view, create, edit, and delete users within a project.</Note>
